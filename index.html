DUI Defense

A DUI Arrest Is Not
a Conviction

Many DUI cases have significant vulnerabilities — improper traffic stops, faulty breathalyzer calibration, chain of custody issues, and more. You have 10 days from your arrest to request a DMV hearing. Call us immediately.

01
DMV Hearing Request (10 Days) We file immediately to challenge your license suspension and protect your driving privileges.
02
Evidence Investigation We obtain dashcam footage, body cam recordings, and calibration records to build your defense.
03
Aggressive Negotiation & Trial We negotiate for dismissals or reduced charges, and we're fully prepared to take your case to trial.
DUI Defense Details

⚠️ Don't Wait — Act Now
