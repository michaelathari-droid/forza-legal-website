Lead Attorney
Michael Athari
Founding Attorney — Forza Legal Defense, PC

Michael Athari founded Forza Legal Defense, PC with an objective to provide his community with representation that is zealous and uncompromising. His time spent as a prosecutor before moving to defense, allows him to bring an uncommon dual perspective to every case.

Michael Athari has personally handled hundreds of DUI cases as a prosecutor, which allowed him to gain deep insight into how law enforcement investigates, how prosecutors build their cases, and, most importantly, where those cases fall apart. He is able to use that knowledge and experience now in defense of his clients.

Michael Athari is licensed to practice law throughout California and has successfully represented clients facing DUI charges and numerous other charges in in Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside, San Bernardino, and San Diego Counties.
